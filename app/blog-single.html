Here’s some tips on how to use Siri to set up your alarms, either at specific times or specific amount of time (minutes, hours).

1 / 3

  • Press & Hold Home Key to activate Siri. Say: “Set an alarm for [time].”

  • Siri will set and display your alarm to confirm.

  • Press & Hold Home Key to activate Siri. Say: “Set an alarm in [amount of time].”
